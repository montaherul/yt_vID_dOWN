Local YouTube Downloader

Download videos, playlists, or audio in one place.

Paste a YouTube link, choose the quality you want, and let the app save everything locally with yt-dlp and FFmpeg.

Run python app.py first. If this page is opened directly or from Live Server, it will connect to http://127.0.0.1:5000.

The selected folder will be used for videos, audio files, and playlist folders.

This hosted demo runs on Render. YouTube often blocks cloud-server requests, and downloads save on the server, not on your device. For reliable use, run the app locally.
